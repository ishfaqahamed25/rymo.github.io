About Rymo

Founded in January 2007, Cup of Jo covers style, design, food, culture, travel, relationships and parenting. Our readers are enthusiastic women who love discovering new things. Cup of Jo receives over 5 million monthly page views and 1 million monthly unique visitors.

Cup of Jo regularly donates a percentage of profits to organizations we support, including RAICES, NAACP, Greenpeace, Save the Children, The Florence Project and Room to Grow.

Thank you so much for reading.
